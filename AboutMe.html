ABOUT ME

I am Ricardo Collie from Columbus, Mississippi. As shown above, I am an alumni of Mississippi State University. After college, in 2013, I decided to join the miliatry. After training in Texas, I PCS'd to Colorado Springs, CO. In 2014 and 2017 I deployed to Kuwait and Saudia Arabia. In 2016, I was featured in a video for the Airmen's Magazine at my previous duty station in Colorado Springs (Cheyenne Mountain Air Force Station). In 2018, I decided to enroll into the computer programming technology program at Southwestern College. I will be graduating soon and I will look forward to pursuing a Master's degree. After my military stint I hope to become a programmer/developer.
